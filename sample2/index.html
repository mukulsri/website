Vision

To make students job-ready.

Mission

To train engineering candidates to be ready for on-campus and off-campus placements.

  • Why: There is a significant gap in candidates' skills, both in soft skills and technical skills, as observed through extensive industry experience.
  • Who: Final-year students eligible for placement drives, specifically those in their 7th and 8th semesters.
  • Branches: CS, ECE, IT
  • How: Providing career counseling based on current market trends, assessing individual skills, offering resources for study, and providing personalized guidance.
  • Where: On-campus and virtual training.
  • When: During the 7th and 8th semesters.

How We Operate

Soft Skills Training

  • Interview preparation: Dress code, appearance, and behavior.
  • Resume building: Crafting a professional resume tailored to different job profiles.
  • LinkedIn profile optimization: Creating and maintaining a compelling LinkedIn profile.
  • Written test preparation: Covering quantitative aptitude, reasoning, and verbal skills.
  • Group discussion (GD) preparation: Techniques and practice for group discussions.
  • Confidence building: Activities and workshops to boost self-confidence.

Technical Skills Training

  • Project knowledge: Deep understanding of academic and personal projects.
  • Favorite subject expertise: Building expertise in chosen subjects of interest.
  • Job profile awareness: Understanding different job roles and their requirements.
  • Mock interviews: Simulated interviews to practice and improve.

Steps to Implement

  • Develop a Pitch Deck: Create a presentation to introduce the program to colleges.
  • Team Introduction and Expertise: Showcase the team's skills and experience.
  • Establish Online Presence: Launch LinkedIn page and WordPress site.
  • College Outreach: Partner with colleges for on-campus training sessions and recruit students.

Milestones

  • Pitch Deck: Finalize the pitch deck and brochure.
  • Online Presence: Launch LinkedIn page and WordPress site.
  • Enrollment: Open enrollment through Google Forms.
  • College Engagement: Start filtering and engaging with colleges for the first batch of training.
