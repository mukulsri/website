ABOUT US

Our Mission and Vision

We bridge the skills gap by preparing final-year engineering students for successful placements through both technical and soft skills training. We cater specifically to students in their 7th and 8th semesters from CS, ECE, and IT branches.

Why We Exist: This initiative addresses the skills gap in both technical and soft skills required for placement success.

How We Operate: We provide a comprehensive training process through on-campus and virtual sessions to ensure all-round preparation.

PROGRAMS AND SERVICES

Enhance your skills with our targeted programs

Soft Skills Training

  • Interview Preparation: Tips on dress code, appearance, and behavior.
  • Resume Building: Guidance on crafting a professional resume tailored to job profiles.
  • LinkedIn Profile Optimization: Tips for creating a compelling LinkedIn profile.
  • Written Test Preparation: Training in quantitative aptitude, reasoning, and verbal skills.
  • Group Discussion (GD) Preparation: Techniques and practice for group discussions.
  • Confidence Building: Activities and workshops to boost self-confidence.

Technical Skills Training

  • Project Knowledge: In-depth understanding of academic and personal projects.
  • Favorite Subject Expertise: Building expertise in selected subjects of interest.
  • Job Profile Awareness: Educating on different job roles and their requirements.
  • Mock Interviews: Conducting simulated interviews for practice and improvement.

Career Counseling

Personalized guidance based on market trends and individual assessments to help you navigate your career path.
